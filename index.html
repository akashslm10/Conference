INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON CLIMATE CHANGE,
CULTURAL HERITAGE & SUSTAINABLE HABITAT

The Conference

Welcome to the International Conference on “Climate Change Cultural Heritage & Sustainable Habitat” – Uniting for a Sustainable Future! In a world facing unprecedented environmental challenges, we are proud to invite you to a gathering of brilliant minds and passionate individuals dedicated to addressing one of the most critical issues of our time: climate change, to understand its impact on Cultural Heritage and Habitat. Our conference aims to foster a global dialogue, bringing together climate scientists, urban planners, conservation architects, academicians, activists, researchers and policy makers from every corner of the globe. Together, we will explore innovative solutions, share groundbreaking research, and catalyze collective action to combat the urgent threats posed by climate change on cultural heritage and habitat. This conference will serve as a platform for knowledge exchange, collaboration, and inspiration for building a sustainable future. It is a call to debate and recognize our shared responsibility in safeguarding our planet's future. Join us as we embark on a transformative journey towards a more sustainable and resilient world. Together, we can turn the tide on climate change and its impact on cultural heritage and habitat for forging towards a more significant, brighter, cleaner, and equitable future for generations to come. Be part of the change. Be part of the solution. Welcome to the International Conference on Climate Change, Cultural Heritage, and Sustainable Habitat.

National Institute Of Technology Patna
Patna (Bihar), India

National Institute of Technology Patna is the 18th National Institute of Technology created by the Ministry of H.R.D. Government of India after rechristening the erstwhile Bihar College of Engineering Patna on 28. 01. 2004. NIT Patna marked its humble beginning in 1886 with the establishment of the pleaders' survey training school which was subsequently promoted to Bihar College of Engineering Patna in 1924. This made this institute the 6th Oldest Engineering Institute of India. The institute is situated on the south bank of the holy river Ganges behind Gandhi Ghat, one of the most important and reverential places of Patna.
